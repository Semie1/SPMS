  • Complete system management

Database Structure

Entities

Entity Key Attributes Description
Student student_id, name, dob, gender, admission_date, class_id Stores information about students including personal details and assigned class
Class class_id, name, grade, section, academic_year Represents a class/section in the school with its grade and academic year
Subject subject_id, name, code, description, grade Contains details about subjects taught in the school for different grades
Teacher teacher_id, name, qualification, joining_date, contact Stores information about teachers including their qualifications
Marks mark_id, student_id, subject_id, term_id, marks_obtained, max_marks Records marks obtained by students in different subjects for each term
Attendance attendance_id, student_id, date, status, term_id Tracks daily attendance records of students during each term
Term term_id, name, start_date, end_date, academic_year Defines academic terms within an academic year with their date ranges
User user_id, username, password, role, last_login System users including administrators, teachers, and other staff with access levels

Relationships

Student Class: A student belongs to one class, and a class has many students
Teacher Subject: A teacher can teach multiple subjects, and a subject can be taught by multiple teachers
Class Subject: A class has multiple subjects, and a subject can be taught in multiple classes
Student Marks: A student has many marks records across subjects and terms
Student Attendance: A student has many attendance records across terms
Term Marks: Marks are recorded for specific terms
Term Attendance: Attendance is tracked within specific terms
Subject Marks: Marks are recorded for specific subjects
